Manufacturing Execution System

Computerized systems in manufacturing to track and document the process from raw material to finished goods

    • staff
    • machines
  • function areas
    • product life cycle
    • resource scheduling
    • order execution / dispatch
    • production analysis
    • overall equipment effectiveness
    • product quality
    • track and trace
  • standards and models
    • product definitions
      • storage
      • version control
      • production rules
      • bill of materials/resources
      • recipe data
    • scheduling
      • collection of orders to meet production requirements (from ERP)
    • production orders
      • handle batches / runs
  • execution of orders

  • collection of data
    • storage
    • exchange of process data
    • equipment status
    • material lot information

  • performance analysis

  • track and trace
    • registration/retrieval of information like lots, orders and equipment
  • dump
